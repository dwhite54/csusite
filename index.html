David McNeely-White

Colorado State University · david.white.679@gmail.com

I'm a PhD student studying under Dr. Ross Beveridge. I'm also a research assistant in the CSU Computer Vision Research Group, co-led by Dr. Beveridge and Dr. Bruce Draper.

My work focuses on understanding convolutional neural network (CNN) feature spaces. Most recently, we have learned that different networks learn (almost) the same feature space when trained on the same data. This research will be used in the DARPA Active Interpretation of Disparate Alternatives (AIDA) program. In addition, I contribute to the lab's work under the DARPA Communicating with Computers (CwC) program.
